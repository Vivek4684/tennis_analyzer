⚙️ Setup

  1. Get a free Gemini API key at aistudio.google.com/app/apikey
  2. Set GEMINI_API_KEY in your Render environment variables

🎥 Recording Tips

  • Position camera at the side of the court, mid-court height
  • Use a tripod or fence mount for stable footage
  • Ensure the full court is visible in frame
  • Good lighting improves analysis accuracy

📱 Using the App

  1. Upload your match video (up to 500MB)
  2. Tag each player by clicking their position on the court diagram
  3. Click Analyze Match and wait ~30–60 seconds
  4. Review per-player ratings and coaching feedback

📊 Understanding Results

Ratings are out of 10

Green (7+) — Strong performance
Amber (5–7) — Average, room to improve
Red (<5) — Needs significant work

Feedback is AI-generated and indicative, not professional coaching.
